The Watchmaker's Story

Three Generations of Horological Mastery

Founded by Elias Thorne in 1987, Restore started in a small workshop in the heart of the clockmaker's district. Elias, a former apprentice to the great Swiss masters, wanted to create a haven for vintage timepieces that had been abandoned by common repair shops.

Today, his daughter Juliana and their team of six master watchmakers continue the tradition. We combine original factory tools with the latest timing technology to ensure every piece is restored to museum criteria.
